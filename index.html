My name is Jack Roehr and I am a Boy Scout at Troop 641 Chapelwood. Every year, our troop holds a fundraiser to support Boy Scouts. We sell wonderful holiday decorations that both beautify people's homes for the holiday season and support our troop. All of the greenery is real and live, and it is shipped to you with in the first two weeks after Thanksgiving. It also makes a great Christmas gift for family and friends: it can be shipped to anyone in the contiguous United States for no additional charge. Even if you already have artificial decorations, you should use this for your entire house instead for three great reasons:
  • It looks great. Real live greenery looks so much better than fake stuff. This makes your whole home look better.
  • It smells wonderful. Decorations made from real trees smell wonderful! Artificial junk has no smell, but this greenery fills your home with a wonderful holiday aroma!
  • Best of all, you support the Boy Scouts! We use the proceeds from this for many great things such as camping, scholarships for scouts who otherwise couldn't afford the program, and to support the misson of Scouting: to teach leadership, outdoor skills, and good citizenship.
